🍨 Ice Cream Ingredients

Milk & Cream

The base of most ice creams, providing richness and smooth texture.

Sugar

Sweetens the mixture and helps balance flavors.

Egg Yolks

Used in custard-style ice creams for creaminess and stability.

Flavorings

Includes vanilla, chocolate, fruit purees, nuts, or spices to create variety.

Stabilizers

Ingredients like gelatin or guar gum help maintain texture and prevent ice crystals.

Air

Whipped into the mixture during churning, giving ice cream its lightness.
