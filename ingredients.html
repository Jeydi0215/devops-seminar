What goes in

Nothing fake,
nothing hidden.

Every ingredient we use has a reason to be there. Here's what goes into every batch — and why it matters.

🍨

The ingredients

🥛

Milk & Cream

The base of every batch. We source fresh full-fat cream from a single dairy farm — it gives our ice cream that dense, slow-melt richness you can't fake with substitutes.

🍬

Sugar

Unrefined cane sugar only. It sweetens and balances, but it also lowers the freezing point — that's what keeps the texture scoopable straight from the freezer.

🥚

Egg Yolks

Used in our custard-style bases. The lecithin in yolks acts as a natural emulsifier, binding fat and water into a silky, stable mixture that holds its texture for days.

🍫

Flavorings

Real vanilla pods, Mindanao cacao, seasonal fruit purees, roasted nuts, and fresh herbs. No extracts, no concentrates. If the fruit isn't good right now, we wait.

🌿

Stabilizers

A small amount of locust bean gum — that's it. It slows ice crystal growth so your scoop stays smooth from counter to cone. Nothing synthetic, nothing you can't pronounce.

💨

Air

Churned in slowly at low speed. Less air means a denser scoop — richer on the palate, and it holds up longer on a warm afternoon. We keep our overrun low on purpose.

Where we source

Local dairy

Our cream and milk come from a family-run farm 40 minutes from the shop. We visit every season to check on the herd.

Mindanao cacao

Single-origin chocolate from Davao. We've worked with the same cooperative since 2011 — they know our quality standards by heart.

Seasonal produce

We buy fruit from the Saturday farmers' market. When something's in season, it goes on the menu. When it's not, it doesn't.
